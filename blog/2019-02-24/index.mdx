There's a simple way to get past lyrical writer's block and the dreaded _Blank Page_.

Set a timer for 1 to 5 minutes and write whatever comes to mind. 

This will mainly get out anything that is on your mind, clearing it up for writing. 

But it may also reveal some interesting themes for a song.
